Hello, and welcome to my website!  
I am Dr. Japheth Mursi, an Assistant Professor of Data Science at Daystar University, Coordinator of the Daystar Institute of Data Science and Cybersecurity, and Co-Founder of IwazoLab, a data analytics and research startup based in Nairobi.

My research lies at the intersection of information systems, AI/ML, and computational social science, with an applied focus on digital trust, misinformation, and financial literacy. I employ NLP, behavioral analytics, and large language models to investigate how individuals engage with digital content, and how AI can enhance trust, access, and inclusivity.

My Ph.D. work at the University of KwaZulu-Natal developed a hybrid fuzzy logic and machine learning system for personality prediction on social media, contributing to the early detection of toxic online behavior. I have since led projects at SIMElab Africa addressing misinformation, civic participation, and mental health on social media.

At IwazoLab, we developed Finlingo, a conversational AI platform published at IEEE ICTMOD 2024, offering scam detection and adaptive financial learning.

Over the past six years, I’ve led impactful research projects in both academic and industry-adjacent settings. As Research Lead at SIMElab Africa (2022–2024), I oversaw a 21-person team working on social media misinformation, digital well-being, and maternal health AI prototypes. I'm also the driving force behind Finlingo, which has been presented at global conferences such as IEEE ICTMOD and ASAC.

In addition to teaching courses in business analytics, software engineering, and social media analytics, I’ve led the design of new data science curricula at both Daystar University and USIU-Africa to align with evolving industry and societal needs.

Beyond academia, I serve as a University Council Member at KAG East University, a Sunday School teacher at ICC Kitengela, and a Lead Mentor at NiweBora.

My goal is to bridge research and real-world impact—equipping students, SMEs, and communities with data-driven tools to thrive in a rapidly changing digital landscape.
